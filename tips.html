1. Create a Study Schedule 🕒

Planning homework and study time helps students avoid procrastination and feel less overwhelmed.

2. Stay Hydrated 💧

Drinking enough water helps improve focus, energy, and memory during school.

3. Sleep Enough 😴

Students should try to get at least 8 hours of sleep each night to stay healthy.

4. Take Breaks 🌷

Short breaks between studying can help your brain focus better and reduce stress.
